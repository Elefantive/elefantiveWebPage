## March 4, 2026 - CMS Preview Improvements

Here's what changed earlier this week in your CMS and how you can benefit.

---

## 🎯 Previous Updates

---

## 🎯 What You Can Now Do

### **1. See Product Links Exactly as They'll Appear**
When you add links to your product descriptions (like Amazon links), you can now see them as **clickable blue links in the preview** before you publish.

**How to use it:**
1. Open any product to edit
2. In the "Intro Text" field, add a link like: `[amazon](https://www.amazon.fr/...)`
3. Look at the preview on the right - you'll see it as a proper blue link
4. Click "Publish" when it looks right
5. Your customers will see the same styled link on the website

**What changed:** Previously, you would see raw text like `[amazon](url)` in the preview, but now it displays as a real link.

---

### **2. Homepage Content Now Goes in the Right Place**
When you edit your homepage, any additional text you add now appears in the **grey header box at the top**, not below the products.

**How to use it:**
1. Go to Pages → Homepage
2. Add your content under "Additional Content"
3. The preview shows it right where it will appear on the live site
4. No more surprises about where your text ends up!

**What changed:** Before, content appeared below the product grid in the preview, but on the actual website it appeared in the header. Now the preview matches reality.

---

### **3. Preview the Product Page Footer Before Publishing**
At the bottom of each product page, there's a special footer section. Now you can see exactly how it looks with your text **and the action buttons** before publishing.

**How to use it:**
1. Go to Pages → Product Footer
2. Edit the text that appears at the bottom of all product pages
3. The preview shows your text with the styling, border, and buttons
4. You can see exactly how it will look to customers

**What changed:** This footer section had no preview before, so you couldn't see how it looked until it was published.

---

### **4. Language Switcher Stays Still**
The language buttons (FR - EN - DE - RO) at the top of the website **always stay in the same position** now. When you switch languages, only the highlight changes - much cleaner!

**How to use it:**
1. Simply click any language button to switch
2. Watch how the button highlight moves, but the position stays the same
3. Much less visual jumping and disruption

**What changed:** Previously, buttons would shuffle around when you switched languages, making the interface feel unstable.

---

### **5. Check "What's New" Anytime in the CMS**
There's now a new button in your CMS called **"📣 What's New"** in the bottom-right corner.

**How to use it:**
1. Log into your CMS at elefantive.com/admin
2. Click the "📣 What's New" button in the bottom-right corner
3. A popup shows all the latest updates and improvements
4. Click the X button to close it

**What changed:** You now have a central place to check what's been updated without searching for documentation.
